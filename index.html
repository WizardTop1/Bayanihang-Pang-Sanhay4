Bayanihang Pang Sanhay


Bayanihan Pang Sanhay is an online platform dedicated to connecting donors with students in need at Agusan National High School. By fostering a spirit of unity and mutual aid, this platform allows community members to easily contribute school supplies, food, and clothing to support students facing resource challenges. Through the power of ICT, Bayanihan Pang Sanhay simplifies donation matching, making it easier for everyone to lend a helping hand and build a stronger, more supportive community.
